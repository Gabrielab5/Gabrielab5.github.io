About Me

My journey into software engineering began in high school, where I successfully completed an intensive 10-unit Computer Science track, culminating in a final project developing a chess game in C#. This experience solidified my passion for the logic and creativity of development. After completing my military service in the Israel Defense Forces, I immediately pursued my career goals by enrolling at Shamoon College of Engineering. I am now set to graduate with a B.Sc. in Software Engineering, fully prepared to apply my academic knowledge and problem-solving skills to a professional role.

As a software engineer, I possess a comprehensive passion for the field. My core strengths lie in three key areas: developing aesthetically pleasing and functional user interfaces, architecting solutions to complex algorithmic challenges, and working with data to extract meaningful insights. I am a highly adaptable engineer, confident in my ability to tackle any task required to see a project through to success.

In addition to my technical pursuits, I am committed to personal and professional growth. I challenge myself physically through sports and stay current with the ever-evolving tech landscape by following AI news and industry summaries. I also find great fulfillment in technology education and outreach.

I am eager to begin my professional journey and am seeking a full-time position as a Junior Software Engineer. I am confident that my dedication and versatile skill set would be a valuable asset to your team.
